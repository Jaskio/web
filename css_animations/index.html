Preparation

Form with ingredients the mixture into 4 patties (each will be ¼ lb.), and set aside.

Cooking

Heat a large skillet or griddle over high heat and add ½ tbsp. olive oil. Once hot, add the patties and sear until golden brown on each side, about 1-2 minutes per side. Then, turn the heat to medium-high and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes per side. op each burger with a slice of gorgonzola cheese and Reduce heat; once is melted, remove from heat.

Assembly

Spread 1-2 tbsp. pesto on the inside of the buns. Then, on the bottom bun, place the patty and add some extra pine nuts. Place the top bun on top and serve.
